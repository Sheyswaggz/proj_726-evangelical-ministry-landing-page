Our Mission

At Grace Community Church, we are dedicated to sharing the Gospel of Jesus Christ and building a loving community of believers rooted in faith, hope, and service.

Proclaim

Sharing the Good News of salvation through Jesus Christ with boldness and compassion.

Nurture

Fostering spiritual growth through biblical teaching, discipleship, and fellowship.

Serve

Demonstrating Christ's love through compassionate service to our community and world.

About Our Ministry

Who We Are

Grace Community Church is a vibrant evangelical ministry committed to glorifying God and making disciples of Jesus Christ. We are a diverse family of believers united by our love for the Lord and our desire to see lives transformed by the Gospel.

Since our founding, we have been blessed to witness countless lives changed through the power of God's Word, the fellowship of believers, and the work of the Holy Spirit in our midst.

What We Believe

  • The Bible is the inspired and authoritative Word of God
  • Salvation is found in Jesus Christ alone through faith
  • The Holy Spirit empowers believers for godly living
  • The Church is called to worship, fellowship, and service
  • Christ will return to establish His eternal kingdom
